ALFA Web-based Toolset¶

The ALFA web-based IDE delivers a complete web-based experience to working with ALFA files.

Working with GIT/SVN/CVS or a Source Control System of your choice to manage your models, all ALFA tools are accessed via the browser in your workspace. Workspaces are maintained on a centrally hosted server (cloud or on-premise).

In addition to IDE/editing, other features will be added alongside the IDE such as live model documentation, model release management etc.

For further information, please contact info@schemarise.com or goto www.schemarise.com for details.
