dateDiff
========

Get the duration between 2 temporal values.

__Parameters:__

|No.|Type  |Comments|
|---|------|--------|
|1  |:ref:`dateType`|1st date value|
|2  |:ref:`dateType`|2nd date value|


__Returns:__

`long` - number of days between the 2 dates


__Usages:__

>- `dateDiff( d1:date, d2:date) : long`
