toDatetime
==========

Convert `string` to `datetime`.

__Parameters:__

|No.|Type  |Comments|
|--------|----------|--------|
|1|:ref:`stringType`|String conforming to ISO-8601 `yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss` format|

__Returns:__

`datetime` - The value as a datetime.

__Usages:__

>- `toDatetime( v:string ) : datetime`
