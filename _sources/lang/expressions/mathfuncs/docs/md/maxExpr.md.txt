max
===

Get the maximum of 2 numbers.

__Parameters:__

|No.     |Type       |Comments |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| 1 |Any numeric type|1st value|
| 1 |Any numeric type|2nd value|

__Returns:__

`Any numeric type` - the max value of the 2 inputs

__Usages:__
>- `max( a : numeric-type, b : numeric-type ) : numeric-type`
