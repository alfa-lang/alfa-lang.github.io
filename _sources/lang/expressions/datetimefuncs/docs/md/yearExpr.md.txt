year
====

Get the 'year' value from a :ref:`dateType` or :ref:`datetimeType`.

__Parameters:__

|No.|Type  |Comments|
|---|------|--------|
|1  |:ref:`dateType`, :ref:`datetimeType` or :ref:`durationType`|Value to extract year from|

__Returns:__

`short(0,23)` - A :ref:`shortType` value representing the year.


__Usages:__

>- `year( d:date) : short`
>- `year( d:datetime) : short`
