day
===

Get the 'day' value from a :ref:`dateType` or :ref:`datetimeType`.

__Parameters:__

|No.|Type  |Comments|
|---|------|--------|
|1  |:ref:`dateType`, :ref:`datetimeType` or :ref:`durationType`|Value to extract day from|


__Returns:__

`short(1,31)` - A :ref:`shortType` value representing the day.


__Usages:__

>- `day( d:date) : short`
>- `day( d:datetime) : short`
